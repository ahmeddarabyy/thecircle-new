Coworking Spaces in Alexandria

The Circle offers flexible coworking spaces in Alexandria with locations in Kafr Abdo and Roushdy. Our workspaces are designed for freelancers, startups, and companies building remote teams in Egypt. Explore our private offices, dedicated desks, shared spaces, and back offices.

General & Location

We have two premium locations in Alexandria: a historic villa in Kafr Abdo and a corporate floor in Roushdy.

Our spaces are accessible 24/7 for monthly members.

Pricing varies depending on the type of space you need. Daily shared spaces start at affordable rates, while private offices and dedicated desks are billed monthly. Check our booking tool for exact pricing.
