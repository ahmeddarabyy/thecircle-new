Our Story

We built the workspace
we always wished existed.

The Circle was founded on one idea: that professionals in Alexandria deserve world-class workspaces that match their ambition.

Our Mission

Building the most
productive places
in Alexandria.

When we started The Circle, we saw a big problem for teams in Alexandria, Egypt. Most companies were stuck between working out of small, messy rooms or signing long-term leases that cost too much money and time.

We bridged this gap by creating beautiful, ready-to-use private offices and meeting rooms that don't need a 3-year commitment or a huge upfront cost. We make it easy for teams to just move in, enjoy a great design, and focus on their work instead of managing a building in either our Kafr Abdo or Roushdy branches.

Why Alexandria Is Becoming a Hub for Remote Teams

Alexandria is quickly becoming one of Egypt’s fastest-growing cities for startups, remote workers, and international companies. With lower operational costs compared to Cairo and access to top talent from Alexandria’s universities, many companies are choosing to open offices here.

By setting up a back office or dedicated desk at The Circle, you tap directly into this growing ecosystem.
