Professional Meeting Rooms in Alexandria

The Circle offers fully equipped meeting rooms in Alexandria designed for client meetings, team workshops, and presentations. Our spaces are located in Kafr Abdo and Roushdy, two of the city's most accessible business districts.

Each meeting room includes high-speed internet, presentation screens, whiteboards, and guest reception services. Rooms can be booked by the hour or for a full day.

Meeting Room Sizes

  • Small meeting rooms for 4–6 people
  • Conference rooms for 8–10 people
  • Boardrooms for executive meetings

Meeting Rooms at Our Alexandria Locations

Our meeting rooms are available at both of The Circle locations in Alexandria:

  • Kafr Abdo – quiet villa workspace
  • Roushdy – central business district

Meeting Rooms for Companies and Remote Teams

Companies operating in Alexandria often need a professional place to host meetings with clients or remote team members. The Circle provides flexible meeting rooms without the need to rent and manage traditional office space.
