01 / The Epicenter

More than an office.
An epicenter.

We established our headquarters on Syria Street because of its undeniable energy. Roushdy is the premium business district of Alexandria. By placing The Circle right in the middle of it, we ensured our members were never more than a few minutes away from clients, transport, and the city’s best amenities.

02 / Scaling Space

Built to scale with you.

The interior architecture of Roushdy reflects the pace of the companies that operate within it. We favored open, dynamic layouts with industrial undertones, emphasizing connectivity. The layout actively encourages crossing paths with other founders, developers, and creatives.

Whether you are a solo freelancer launching your first product or a growing team establishing a permanent back-office, Roushdy scales with you. We equipped this location with multiple private team rooms, expansive shared desking areas, and robust IT infrastructure capable of handling the highest enterprise demands.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there parking available at the Roushdy branch?

Yes, street parking is available on Syria Street and the surrounding side streets. There are also several commercial parking lots located within a 5-minute walk of our building.

What are the operating hours for the Roushdy location?

The Roushdy branch is open from 9:00 AM to 12:00 AM (midnight), seven days a week, providing maximum flexibility for teams with different schedules.

Is the Roushdy branch suitable for high-growth corporate teams?

Absolutely. Roushdy is specifically designed for B2B operations, offering large-format private suites and full-floor back-office solutions for teams of 20 or more professionals.
