The Heritage Location

Coworking Space in Kafr Abdo, Alexandria

Where Alexandria's deep architectural history meets the future of flexible execution.

Book a tour
01 / Heritage

Built in the early 1900s by English architects.

We chose Kafr Abdo not just for its prestigious tree-lined streets, but for the inherent character of the buildings that stand here. This standalone villa provided the perfect canvas: grand ceiling heights, sweeping natural light, and a footprint that allowed us to create distinct zones of deep focus and community collision.

02 / Sanctuary

Quiet environment.
Intense productivity.

We merged the historic architectural integrity with high-performance modern infrastructure. The result is a space that feels deeply organic, offering a serene escape from the noise of the city, complete with a private garden workspace that acts as a natural oasis for our members.

The 24/7 access model here was explicitly designed for professionals who do not conform to traditional hours and demand a secure, inspiring environment at any time of day or night.

Kafr Abdo Branch FAQ

Is there parking in Kafr Abdo?

Yes, there is convenient street parking available nearby, as well as several dedicated parking areas within a short walking distance from our villa.

Yes, The Circle Kafr Abdo offers secure 24/7 access exclusively for our monthly members.

Can companies rent offices here?

Yes, we offer fully furnished premium private offices suitable for teams of various sizes.

Absolutely. We have professionally equipped meeting rooms that can be booked by both members and non-members online.
