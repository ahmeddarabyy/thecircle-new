Published March 30, 2026 • 4 min read

When searching for an office for rent in Alexandria, you will face a critical decision: should you sign a traditional commercial lease or opt for a coworking space membership? In 2026, the answer depends on your team's size and your need for agility.

Breaking Down the Costs
