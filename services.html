Flexible Workspace Services in Alexandria

The Circle provides a comprehensive range of workspace services across Alexandria, Egypt. Whether you need a private office for your growing team, a reliable meeting room to impress clients, or a dedicated desk to call your own, our two locations in Kafr Abdo and Roushdy are equipped with enterprise-grade infrastructure and an unmatched level of service.

Private Offices in Alexandria

Our private offices are the flagship product at The Circle. Fully furnished, acoustically isolated, and wired for enterprise networking, these offices serve startups, agencies, satellite teams, and professionals who demand privacy and a premium working environment. Teams of 2 to 15 people operate from our private offices every day without the headache of commercial lease negotiations, contractor delays, or utility management.

Corporate Suites for Large Teams

For companies needing a permanent Alexandria extension office or regional hub, our private suites provide a fully operational corporate environment for teams of 20 or more. These large-format suites come with dedicated infrastructure, customizable layouts, and reception services—delivering a ready-to-deploy back office within 48 hours of signing.

Back Office Solutions in Alexandria

The Circle also specializes in back office solutions designed for companies opening operations, customer support, or engineering departments in Alexandria. By choosing a managed back office space rather than a traditional lease, you eliminate fit-out costs, IT procurement headaches, and the complexity of managing a remote facility.

Shared Coworking Spaces & Hot Desks

For freelancers, remote workers, and students, our shared coworking spaces offer an open, vibrant environment with daily and monthly access options. Plug into high-speed fiber Wi-Fi, unlimited premium coffee, and a curated professional community—all in flagship locations in Kafr Abdo and Roushdy.

Dedicated Desks for Professionals

Our dedicated desks are the ideal upgrade from hot desking. Unlike shared spaces, your dedicated desk is exclusively yours around the clock, seven days a week, with 24/7 building access. Leave your external monitors, mechanical keyboard, and reference documents right where they are—your workspace is always ready.

Meeting Rooms by the Hour

All members—and even non-members—can book our meeting rooms on demand. Equipped with 4K presentation screens, whiteboards, and high-speed internet, our conference rooms serve client pitches, board meetings, team workshops, and corporate training sessions. Small spaces for 4–6 people and large boardrooms for 12–20 people are available at both our Kafr Abdo and Roushdy locations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a private office and a dedicated desk?

A private office is a fully enclosed, lockable space for your team. A dedicated desk is a permanently assigned personal desk in an open shared environment—ideal for solo professionals who want consistency without requiring a full room.

Can I tour the space before committing?

Absolutely. We encourage everyone to schedule a free tour of whichever location interests you—Kafr Abdo or Roushdy—so you can experience the environment firsthand before making a decision.

Are there long-term contracts?

No. All of our workspace services operate on flexible monthly terms. You are never locked into a multi-year lease. This is one of the core advantages of choosing The Circle over traditional commercial real estate in Alexandria.
