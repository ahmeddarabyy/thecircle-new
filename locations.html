Frequently Asked Questions

How many branches does The Circle have in Alexandria?
The Circle currently operates two premium locations in Alexandria: our boutique villa branch in Kafr Abdo and our modern commercial branch on Syria Street in Roushdy.
Which Circle location is best for teams?
Both locations accommodate teams perfectly. Kafr Abdo offers a quiet, standalone villa environment, while Roushdy puts you right in the center of the corporate district.
Do both locations offer private offices?
Yes, both our Kafr Abdo and Roushdy workspaces have premium furnished private offices ready for move-in.
Do both locations offer meeting rooms?
Yes, both our Kafr Abdo and Roushdy locations are equipped with high-tech meeting rooms featuring 4K displays and enterprise-level conferencing facilities.
Are the workspaces open 24/7?
Yes, any monthly member gets secure 24/7 access to their home location, so you can work whenever you need to.
Which location is closer to central Alexandria?
Our Roushdy headquarters on Syria Street is located directly on a major artery, making it ultra-accessible from all central districts in Alexandria.
Can I use my membership at both locations?
Yes, our memberships are designed for maximum flexibility. All members have reciprocal access to both our Kafr Abdo and Roushdy branches, allowing you to work from the location that's most convenient for your day.
