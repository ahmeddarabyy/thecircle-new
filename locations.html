Frequently Asked Questions

Which Circle location is best for teams?

Both locations accommodate teams perfectly. Kafr Abdo offers a quiet, standalone villa environment, while Roushdy puts you right in the center of the corporate district.

Do both locations offer private offices?

Yes, both our Kafr Abdo and Roushdy workspaces have premium furnished private offices ready for move-in.

Are the workspaces open 24/7?

Yes, any monthly member gets secure 24/7 access to their home location, so you can work whenever you need to.

Which location is closer to central Alexandria?

Our Roushdy headquarters on Syria Street is located directly on a major artery, making it ultra-accessible from all central districts in Alexandria.
