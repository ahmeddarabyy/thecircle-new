Coworking Spaces in Alexandria

The Circle offers flexible coworking spaces in Alexandria designed for remote teams, freelancers, and companies building a presence in Egypt.

Choose between our historic Kafr Abdo villa or our Roushdy headquarters located on Syria Street. Both locations provide fast internet, professional meeting rooms, and comfortable shared workspaces.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book my desk in advance?

While walk-ins to our Alexandria spaces are welcome subject to availability, we highly recommend booking your hot desk online via this page to guarantee your workspace prior to your arrival.

Are meeting rooms included in shared space day passes?

Meeting rooms are booked separately to ensure private, uninterrupted blocks for teams. You can securely reserve hourly slots by choosing "Meeting Room" as your space type in the booking portal above.
